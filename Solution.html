What is the role of sourcing agent in India?

1

Verify the supplier credentials and visit the factory

Our on-the-ground presence in India means our team can verify ‘who is
who’ and separate 'the good from the bad,’ enabling your business to make
accurate sourcing decisions. Moreover, when you do business with us, you
can be assured that all payments will always be handled safely and
reliably. As your sourcing agent, you won’t ever need to worry about scams
and frauds.

2

Source from factories you can’t find online

70% of products we export are not listed on Alibaba, IndiaMart, or other
B2B websites. As one example, many factories will only attend trade shows
in India for their industry. Often, these are some of the best manufacturers
in their field.

3

Save time and avoid mistakes

Sourcing in the right region, understanding different specifications and
materials, and knowing how and when to get the best price from a
potential manufacturer are just a few of the considerations we make to
save our customers time and avoid any unnecessary pitfalls along the way.

4

Mitigate the risk of potential fraud

We approach each assignment as a mutual obligation between the customer
and ourselves, rather than a non-commitment agreement. Many sourcing
agencies’ strategies are centered on a no-cure/no-pay rapid commission
trade; just quick picks and choices. We ensure quality and reliability.

5

Control quality and logistics

With our highly distributed operation team consisting of full-time employees
and contractors in different regions, we are able to dispatch them to conduct
on-site inspections to evaluate suppliers easily. The same goes for product
inspection during shipment.
